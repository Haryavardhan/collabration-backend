CAREER GUIDANCE KNOWLEDGE BASE
================================

This document is used by an AI-powered chatbot for career guidance, course recommendation,
mentor suggestion, and team-based collaborative learning.

The chatbot assists students from 10th class to degree level.

------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: CAREER GUIDANCE FOR 10TH CLASS STUDENTS
------------------------------------------------

After 10th class, students can choose different streams based on interest and aptitude.

Major options after 10th:
- MPC (Maths, Physics, Chemistry): Suitable for engineering, architecture, and technology careers.
- BiPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry): Suitable for medical, pharmacy, and life science careers.
- Commerce: Leads to CA, CMA, BBA, MBA, finance, and business careers.
- Arts/Humanities: Leads to law, civil services, journalism, psychology, and social sciences.
- Polytechnic/Diploma: Skill-oriented technical education with faster employment options.
- ITI (Industrial Training Institute): Practical skill-based jobs in electrical, mechanical, and trades.

Common mistakes after 10th:
- Choosing a stream due to peer pressure
- Selecting courses without understanding future scope
- Relying on rumors or unverified information

The chatbot helps students choose streams based on interests, strengths, and long-term goals.

------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5: MENTOR GUIDANCE
------------------------------------------------

Mentors play a key role in student success.

Mentor roles:
- Guide students in career decisions
- Review projects
- Provide real-world insights
- Help students avoid wrong career paths

The system recommends mentors based on:
- Course expertise
- Student interests
- Team requirements

Students can request mentors and mentors can accept or reject requests.

------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: TEAM-BASED LEARNING
------------------------------------------------

Collaborative learning improves understanding and motivation.

Features:
- Students can create teams
- Students can request to join teams
- Mentors can guide teams
- Real-time collaboration using chat, whiteboard, and video calls

Benefits:
- Peer learning
- Better communication skills
- Practical problem solving
- Industry-style teamwork

------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: AI CHATBOT ROLE
------------------------------------------------

The AI chatbot provides:
- Career guidance
- Course recommendations
- Mentor suggestions
- Clarification of doubts
